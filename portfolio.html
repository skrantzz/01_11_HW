about me

Hiyyaaa, I'm Sydney. I'm from northern NJ, and I studied photography at MassArt in Boston. I moved here in July 2018 because I wanted to change things up. Photography is my #1 passion, but I'm constantly learning new things and challenging myself. The commercial photography industry is not kind to artists, so I wanted to expand my skill set and have another means of income. I've thought up a few app ideas in the past, but never had the means to bring them to life, so instead of waiting around, I decided to take things into my own hands!
